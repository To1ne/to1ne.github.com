bio

This passion kept growing and that's why he finished his education of Master in Electronics – option ICT – in 2008. Currently Toon is employed as an embedded Linux programmer (C/C++), but he has a great interest for the web. So in his time off he likes to explore the web and work on several web-related hobby projects. Occasionally you can read a new post on his blog.
